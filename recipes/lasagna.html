Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the Lasagna in the order of Meat sauce, Noodles, Ricotta mixture, Mozzarella slices, Meat sauce, Parmesan cheese. Repeat and then top it off with the remaining Parmesan
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
